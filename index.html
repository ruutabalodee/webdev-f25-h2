The Swedish Origin

The Husqvarna story is one of the world's longest-running motorcycle manufacturers, with roots tracing back to a 17th-century weapons foundry founded in Huskvarna, Sweden. The company officially began producing motorcycles in 1903, starting with motorized bicycles. This early venture established a reputation for robust, lightweight, and reliable machines, setting the stage for a century of innovation on two wheels.
